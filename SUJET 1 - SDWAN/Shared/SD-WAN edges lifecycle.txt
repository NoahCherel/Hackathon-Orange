What is new in the November, 2024 Update:Adds a note that EOS/EOL dates are different for China and a customer should reach out toa China based representative for those dates.What is new in the September, 2024 Update:Adds Edge models 510-LTE, 610-LTE, 620 Wi-Fi, 620N Non-Wi-Fi, 640N Non-Wi-Fi, and680N Non-Wi-Fi to the End of Sale/End of Support schedule.Each model has an End of Sale date of 04/01/2025 and an End of Support/End of Life dateof 04/01/2030.These models are added to the table in light of the introduction of Edge models 710-5G,720, and 740, which will replace the above models going forward.Updates the Recommended Action table to add the above models and to revise theAlternative Model column with an appropriate 7x0 model.Updates the TAA Compliant table to include new Edge models 710-5G, 720, and 740.These join the Edge 710-W, as the entire Edge 7x0 model line is TAA compliant. Also notesthe additional 6x0 models announced as End of Sale.What is new in the August, 2024 Update:This update includes the revised branding that changes Velocloud SD-WAN to VeloCloudSD-WAN, and changes Velocloud SD-WAN Edge to VeloCloud Edge.Edge models 510 Wi-Fi, 510N, 610 Wi-Fi, and 610N have all reached End of Sale, eachmodel carries a note that there remains a surplus of inventory for Edge 510's and 610's andthese models can still be purchased while supplies last.Added two new notes under Please Note section stating that End of Sale Edges may not besupported on all platform software releases going forward. Also that End of Support/End ofLife Edges can be expected to have support on the LTS software release that was availableat the time they became End of Life/End of Support.What was new in the February, 2024 Update:This update adds entries for some versions of the Edge 510 and Edge 610. Specifically,the Edge 510 (Wi-Fi), 510N (Non-Wi-Fi), 610 (Wi-Fi), and 610N (Non-Wi-Fi) versions.As documented under Recommended Action, the above models are all replaced bythe Edge 710-W. The Edge 710-W is TAA compliant and becomes generally available on 01February, 2024.Both LTE versions, the 510-LTE and 610-LTE, along with the Edge 610C are not part of thisEnd of Sale/End of Life update and currently have no End of Sale/End of Life date.
