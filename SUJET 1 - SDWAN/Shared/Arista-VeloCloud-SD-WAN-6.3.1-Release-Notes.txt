What Is in The Release Notes
VeloCloud Release 6.3.1 for Orchestrator introduces two new features for VeloBrain: VeloCloud GenAI
Operator and VeloCloud GenAI Reporting. VeloBrain is a powerful AI operator that leverages generative
AI to streamline operations and provide expert-level guidance, context, and automation for managing
VeloCloud deployments. With this release, VeloCloud SASE™ customers and partners gain access to
VeloCloud's advanced AI-driven Operator and Reporting tools, designed to improve usability and streamline
troubleshooting processes.
The release notes cover the following topics:
Recommended Use
This Orchestrator release is recommended for all customers who require the features and functionality first
made available in Release 6.3.0.
Note:
Customers with on-premises Orchestrators are required to contact Broadcom Technical Support to
validate their upgrade playbook before the upgrade process.
Compatibility
Release 6.3.0 Orchestrators support all previous Arista SD-WAN Edge versions.
New Features and Enhancements
VeloBrain – VeloCloud GenAI Operator
1
 
The VeloBrain GenAI Operator, featuring a chat interface powered by Generative AI, delivers expert-level
guidance, contextual insights, and automation to help customers and partners efficiently operate and optimize
their SASE deployments. With this release, the VeloBrain chat interface will:
• Provide answers to product documentation and knowledge base inquiries, complete with references.
• Offer AI-assisted troubleshooting for selected events to significantly reduce time to resolution.

GenAI Reporting
The VeloCloud GenAI Reporting delivers enterprises detailed insights into Generative AI usage across their
organizations with exclusive, GenAI-focused reports. This feature enhances visibility by providing customers
with in-depth analytics on the adoption of top Generative AI applications within their network, along with
identifying the key contributors driving their usage.
